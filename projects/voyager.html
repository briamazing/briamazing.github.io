Voyager

www.voyager.studio

A couple friends and I found ourselves perpetually pitching new projects to each other and wanted a cohesive brand to publish under. Given our affinity for all things NASA (especially 70s NASA), we decided to brand ourselves after the Voyager 1 and 2 probes. Although most projects are not ready for public viewing, Kerosene and Artemis are two case studies that were shared under the Voyager name.
